[2026-05-25] Mathematics as middle step in adoption curve. Over the last two weeks,
noticeable explosion in discussion of LLMs providing deep insight into mathematics.
Hypothesis: adoption curve runs Software Engineering → Mathematics → Theoretical Physics.
Mathematics is behind software but ahead of physics — close enough to physics in rigor
and formalism that it functions as a leading indicator. Physicist audience can look at
what's happening in math now and see their own near future.
